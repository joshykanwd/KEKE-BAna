Information

In the story "The Beginning After The End" (TBATE), Arthur Leywin is the main protagonist. TBATE is a popular web novel written by TurtleMe. Arthur Leywin starts as a powerful king in his previous life but is reincarnated into a world where magic and mythical creatures exist. In his new life, Arthur is born as a young boy with no memory of his past life. However, he quickly discovers that he possesses extraordinary magical abilities, which sets him on a path of self-discovery and adventure. Throughout the series, Arthur navigates various challenges, makes new friends, and unravels mysteries about his past and the world he now inhabits. Arthur Leywin is known for his intelligence, determination, and willingness to protect those he cares about. As the story progresses, he grows in strength and knowledge, eventually becoming a formidable force in the magical realm.

Overall, Arthur Leywin is a complex and compelling character who drives the narrative of TBATE forward with his journey of growth, exploration, and self-realization.
